About Soltown

Soltown is a competitive A Cappella ensemble from Germantown, Wisconsin, representing Germantown High School in vocal excellence.

A Little Nerding Out About Our Name

SolTown: Noun.
  1. Germantown Wisconsin is a small village North of Milwaukee, Wisconsin, commonly referred to as "GTown"
  2. When using the fixed-do system of sightreading, "G" is given the solfeggio syllable "Sol"
  3. The result is that GTown = SolTown

Our Motto: "Always Dominant"

This is not a boastful statement, but rather a comment on the fact that in music theory, "Sol" is the 5th scale degree which is given the name "Dominant" based on the hierarchy of pitches in the Ionic Mode.
